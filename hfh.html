Menstruation myths abound, with some suggesting that it negatively affects bone health in women, but this is largely a misconception.
In fact, menstruation is a natural process and does not directly impact bone health. However, it is important for women to maintain overall health, including a balanced diet rich in calcium and vitamin D, which are essential for bone health.
