According to a report by the National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) in 2017-18, financial constraints were a significant factor preventing students from pursuing higher education in India. The report highlighted that approximately 30% of students in the age group of 18-23 years were unable to continue their education due to financial reasons. Therefore, it is vital to understand the importance of scholarships and also how scholarship awards can help a student in completing his education in the best possible way.
